Mechanism for Removal

If you are a copyright owner and your material has been included in our digital collections, you can request that they be removed from public view. Send a message to diglib@indiana.edu. Please include your name, contact information, and a complete description of the material including the date of the item in question.

Online Citation Format

To cite a text from the Wright American Fiction Project, follow the MLA format:

Cummins, Maria S. Haunted Hearts. Boston: J.E. Tilton, 1864. Wright American Fiction Project. Indiana University Libraries, n.d. Web. 6 September 2013. <http://purl.dlib.indiana.edu/iudl/wright/VAC6202>

Note the latest retrieved date of online text and use the persistent URL for citing the text found in the footer of the full text view or the top of the full text view, as part of "Document Information."
